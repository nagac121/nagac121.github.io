# Transitioning from Full-Stack to Agentic AI

Over the past 14 years, I've built a career in full-stack development, working with technologies like React, Node.js, and various backend frameworks. Recently, I've been diving into the world of Agentic AI, and it's been a fascinating shift.

## What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can act autonomously, making decisions and taking actions on behalf of users. Unlike traditional AI that responds to queries, agentic AI can plan, reason, and execute complex tasks.

## My Experience So Far

LangGraph has been particularly interesting. It's a framework for building stateful, multi-actor applications with LLMs. The ability to create workflows where different agents can collaborate is powerful.

## Future Outlook

I'm excited about how agentic AI will change software development. The combination of traditional programming with AI agents opens up new possibilities for creating more intelligent and adaptive applications.
